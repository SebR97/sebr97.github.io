Who am I?

I'm a data scientist currently working at a South African FinTech Scale-Up while completing my MSc in Actuarial Science & Quantitative Finance, from which I will graduate in December 2021. After spending a few months in 2020 interning as a junior data scientist I realized my passion (addiction?) for translating business problems from words into code and then back into words. I have a genuine curiosity in all things ML with a particular allure towards the fraud detection and prevention domain and a demonstrable history of delivering accurate, scalable and efficient ML-based fraud management solutions for some of South Africa's leading banks and payments services providers.
